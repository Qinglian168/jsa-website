Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between OS2, OM3, and OM4 fiber?

OS2 is single-mode (9/125µm) for long distances up to 100km. OM3/OM4 are multi-mode (50/125µm) for data center short-reach — OM4 supports 100G up to 150m, OM3 up to 100m.

Can you make custom length fiber assemblies?

Yes, we offer custom-cut cables to exact length specifications with certified test reports included.

Do you provide polarity and pin configuration guidance for MTP/MPO?

Our engineers provide free polarity design assistance and can label all MTP/MPO assemblies per your network design documentation.
