Frequently Asked Questions

What types of switches does JSA Solution supply?

We supply a comprehensive range of network switches from Cisco, Huawei, H3C, Ruijie, and more — including Layer 2/3 managed switches, PoE switches (up to 90W), 10G/25G/100G data center switches, and modular chassis switches.

What is the difference between Layer 2 and Layer 3 switches?

Layer 2 switches forward traffic based on MAC addresses within a VLAN, while Layer 3 switches can route traffic between VLANs using IP addresses. For most enterprise networks, Layer 3 switches are recommended for core/distribution layers. Our team can help you choose the right switch type.

Do you carry PoE switches for IP cameras and WiFi APs?

Yes! We carry PoE (Power over Ethernet) switches from all major brands — supporting IEEE 802.3af (15.4W), 802.3at (30W), and 802.3bt (up to 90W). Perfect for IP cameras, wireless APs, VoIP phones, and IoT devices.

Can I buy a single switch or do I need to order in bulk?

Both! We accept orders of any quantity — from a single switch for a small office to bulk orders for large deployments. Volume discounts are available for orders of 10+ units.
