Frequently Asked Questions

What video conferencing system is best for a small meeting room?

For huddle rooms (2-6 people), we recommend all-in-one video bars like Yealink A20, Poly Studio X30, or Logitech Rally Bar Mini. These include camera, mic, speaker, and compute in one device — just mount and connect.

Do you supply Microsoft Teams Rooms solutions?

Yes! We carry Microsoft Teams-certified hardware from Yealink, Poly, Logitech, and MAXHUB. We can provide complete Teams Rooms kits including compute, touch console, camera, and audio.

Can you help design a complete meeting room AV system?

Absolutely! Our solutions team can design a full meeting room setup — including display, camera, audio, control system, and cable management. We serve the Shenzhen area with professional installation.

What is the warranty on conference equipment?

All conference equipment comes with manufacturer warranty (typically 1-3 years). Extended warranty and premium support plans are available for enterprise customers.
