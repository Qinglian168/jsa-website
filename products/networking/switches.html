Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between Layer 2 and Layer 3 switches?

Layer 2 switches forward traffic based on MAC addresses within the same VLAN. Layer 3 switches can route traffic between VLANs using IP addresses, making them suitable for inter-VLAN routing at wire speed.

Do you provide PoE switches for IP cameras and APs?

Yes, we supply PoE, PoE+, and PoE++ (802.3bt) switches that can power IP cameras, wireless APs, and IP phones directly over Ethernet cables.

Can you help with switch stacking and chassis solutions?

Absolutely. We provide StackWise (Cisco), IRF (H3C), iStack (Huawei), and VSX (Aruba) stacking solutions for high availability and simplified management.
