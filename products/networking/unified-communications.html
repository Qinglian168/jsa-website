Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between IP PBX and traditional PBX?

Traditional PBX uses analog lines and proprietary phones. IP PBX uses VoIP (SIP) and IP phones, offering lower cost, easier management, and advanced features like video calling and mobile integration.

Do your UC solutions support Microsoft Teams and Zoom?

Yes, Poly, Cisco Webex, and Logitech devices are certified for Microsoft Teams and Zoom. Yealink also offers Teams-certified IP phones.

Can you help migrate from legacy phone systems to IP PBX?

Absolutely. We provide migration planning, equipment selection, configuration, and cutover support to ensure a smooth transition from legacy systems to modern IP communications.
