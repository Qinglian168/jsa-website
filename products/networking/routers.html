Frequently Asked Questions

What is SD-WAN and do your routers support it?

SD-WAN (Software-Defined WAN) optimizes WAN traffic using application-aware routing. Cisco, Huawei, and H3C routers we supply all support SD-WAN deployment.

Do you supply both enterprise branch routers and carrier-grade core routers?

Yes, we supply the full range — from SMB branch routers (Cisco RV, H3C MSR) to carrier-grade core routers (Cisco ASR 9000, Huawei NE40E).

Can you help with router configuration and deployment?

Yes, our engineers provide pre-sales configuration guidance, and we can arrange professional services for on-site deployment if needed.
