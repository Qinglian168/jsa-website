Frequently Asked Questions

Do you provide calibration and certification for test equipment?

Yes, we offer annual calibration services for Fluke, EXFO, and Viavi equipment through certified labs with traceable certificates.

Can I rent OTDRs or fusion splicers for short-term projects?

Yes, we offer flexible rental programs for test equipment and fusion splicers — daily, weekly, or project-based terms.

Which OTDR wavelength is right for my network?

For single-mode fiber use 1310/1550nm; for multi-mode use 850/1300nm. Our team provides free wavelength selection guidance based on your fiber plant.
