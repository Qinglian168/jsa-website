Complete AV and video conferencing solutions for meeting rooms of all sizes — from huddle rooms to boardrooms. Microsoft Teams and Zoom certified.

Hybrid meeting participants feeling disconnected

Our Approach

1

Room Assessment

Evaluate room size, acoustics, lighting, and usage patterns to determine the right solution.

2

Platform Selection

Choose the best video platform (Teams, Zoom, etc.) and certified hardware for your environment.

3

AV Design

Design the complete audio-visual system including camera, microphone, speaker, display, and control.

4

Installation & Integration

Professional installation with cable management, system integration, and room control panel setup.

Training & Support

User training for easy operation, plus ongoing technical support and equipment maintenance.
